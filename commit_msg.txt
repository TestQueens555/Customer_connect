fix: increase CI timeouts in ActionQueuePage - grid renders slow in headless

Root cause: KendoUI grid and Resolution Desk render after domcontentloaded in CI headless Chromium, causing 10s timeout on [role="group"] and text=RESOLUTION DESK locators.

Fix:
- navigateToActionQueue: wait for heading first (15s), then grid group with 25s timeout + fallback to [role="grid"]
- navigateToActionQueueDetails: wait for h1 first (15s), then RESOLUTION DESK with 25s timeout + fallback to APPROVE button
- clickPerformAction: same pattern as navigateToActionQueueDetails
